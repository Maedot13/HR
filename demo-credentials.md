## Notes

- All accounts have `isTempPassword: true` — the system will prompt for a password change on first login.
- To skip the change-password prompt during a live demo, update `isTempPassword = false` directly in the DB.
- The seed script `prisma/seed-demo.ts` is **fully idempotent** — re-running safely skips existing records.
- To re-seed from scratch, delete employees whose `employeeId` starts with `MAIN-2026-001` or `TECH-2026-00`, then re-run the seeder.
